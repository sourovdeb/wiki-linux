## Lesson 1: keep templates small

Compact templates produce cleaner structured outputs and are easier to maintain.

## Lesson 2: enforce review checkpoints

Even good models drift. Human review before publication is mandatory for educational quality.

## Lesson 3: audit every mutation

Content workflows need a reliable trail of who changed what and when.
