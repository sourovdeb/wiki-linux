# 5 Things I Learned Teaching EFL in Réunion

After completing my CELTA certification and teaching English to adults and kids in
Saint-Pierre La Réunion, here are the five biggest lessons:

**1. Context beats vocabulary lists every time.**
Students remember words they used in a real conversation, not ones they copied from a worksheet.

**2. Silence is a teaching tool.**
Waiting 5 extra seconds after asking a question doubles the quality of responses.

**3. Lesson plans should be frameworks, not scripts.**
The best lessons I ever ran were the ones I adapted live based on what the class needed.

**4. Low-tech often outperforms high-tech.**
A whiteboard and good questions beat any app for building speaking confidence.

**5. Your energy is contagious.**
If you're bored by your own lesson, your students will be too.
